## Company Overview
Backblaze (Nasdaq: BLZE), founded 2007 and headquartered in San Mateo, CA, is a publicly traded cloud storage company that sells low-cost, S3-compatible object storage. It runs two businesses: B2 Cloud Storage (usage-based IaaS object storage sold to developers, ISVs, MSPs, neoclouds, and enterprises) and Computer Backup (subscription backup for individuals and businesses). It monetizes via usage-based storage/egress fees and recurring subscriptions, positioning itself as a no-lock-in, ~80% cheaper alternative to hyperscalers, and is aggressively moving upmarket toward AI/neocloud workloads. It serves 500,000+ customers across 175 countries.
