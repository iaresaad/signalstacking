## Company Overview
Aampe is a San Francisco-headquartered (fully distributed across ~14 countries), agentic-AI infrastructure company founded in 2020 that assigns a dedicated, autonomous AI agent to every individual end-user of a consumer brand. Using reinforcement learning (Thompson Sampling / multi-armed bandits with individual-level causal inference), each agent continuously decides the best message, timing, channel, and frequency for that one user — replacing rigid segmentation and manual A/B testing across push, email, SMS, in-app, and WhatsApp. It sells B2B to marketing, growth, and product teams at consumer/prosumer tech companies (e.g., Swiggy, Grab, Taxfix, ZenBusiness), monetizing via SaaS subscriptions that plug into a customer's existing CDP and CPaaS.
